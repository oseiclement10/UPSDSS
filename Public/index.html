ABOUT US

UPSDSS stands for University Program Selection Decision Support System

UPSDSS is a project aimed at guiding high school students select the best tetiary program.

The project was conceived by concerns of how some students are influenced into selecting programs they do not really like or programs that do not utilize their strengths.

The system takes into account the performance of the student and the interests of the student.

The system generates questions to determine student interest.

The system also take into consideration the academic requirements of each course, and measures it against studentâ€™s wassce or equivalent scores.

The project was developed by Final Year Computer Science students (Osei Asante Clement and Sulkabe Paul Ntaado) at KNUST Ghana
